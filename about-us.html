About Us

Farming has been discovered to be a very lucrative business, making a lot of people get involved in it. Some of these ones have little or no experience about what they are getting involved in which has led many to losses. Here at Everythingfarm.com, we are dedicated to giving an extensive knowledge of various farming systems(plant and animal farming). We make recommendations of specialists in these various fields of farming to help give starter farmers insight on what is expected throughout their first farming experience and beyond. We also provide readable materials that give a general knowledge of whichever specialization you choose. Some of these areas include: fish farming, poultry, piggery, snail farm, rabbit farm, cassava farm, plantain plantation, cocoa plantation and lots more.
